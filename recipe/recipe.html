Sinigang na baboy

Sinigang na Baboy is the ultimate comfort food! Made with pork, vegetables, and tamarind-flavored broth, it's hearty and delicious on its own or served with steamed rice.

  • 1 kilo of pork
  • 1 pc red onion
  • 2 pcs tomato
  • 250 g water spinach
  • 3 pcs eggplant
  • 2 pc green pepper
  • Pepper corns
  • Fish sauce (Optional)
  • 1 pack of Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix Original

    Procedure of Cooking Sinigang na baboy>

  1. Start to cook by boiling onions and tomato
  2. Add the pork into the pot, and add pepper corns
  3. Cook it for 25 to 30 mins
  4. Add the Sinigang Mix and continue to boiling
  5. Add fish sauce and salt (Optional)
  6. After 10 to 15 mins, add your choices of vegetables
