Bicol Express Recipe

Bicol Express Recipe


Ingredients

Good for 6 servings

  • 2 lbs. pork belly (sliced into strips)
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 cups coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup shrimp paste (or salted Krill)
  • 5 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • 5 pieces Thai chili pepper (chopped)
  • 2 thumbs ginger (minced)
  • 1 piece onion (chopped)
  • 2 pieces Serrano pepper (sliced)
  • 1 cup water (optional)
  • 8 grams Maggi Magic Sarap

Step 1

Sauté Aromatics: In a pot or pan, heat some oil over medium heat. Sauté the garlic, ginger, and onion until fragrant and softened.

Step 2

Cook Pork: Add the pork belly strips to the pot and cook until lightly browned.

Step 3

Add Shrimp Paste: Stir in the shrimp paste and cook for a few minutes until fragrant.

Step 4

Simmer with Coconut Milk: Pour in 2 cups of coconut milk and 1 cup of water (if using). Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and let the pork simmer until tender

Step 5

Add Chili Peppers: Add the Thai chili peppers and Serrano peppers. Adjust the number of peppers based on your desired spice level.

Step 6

Simmer and Thicken: Continue to simmer until the sauce has thickened slightly.

Step 7

Add Coconut Cream: Stir in the coconut cream and simmer for a few more minutes until the sauce has reached your desired consistency.

Step 8

Season and Serve: Season with Maggi Magic Sarap to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.
